TWR-CLOUD-Bern

Two-Way-Ranging-based Cooperative Localization
with an Open UWB Dataset
from Bern-University

Developed by the
Communication and Distributed Systems
research group at the University of Bern

TWR-CLOUD-Bern is an open-access, standardized dataset
for researchers working in the fields of wireless sensor networks (WSN),
collaborative localization, and ultra-wideband (UWB) positioning.
It enables fair and reproducible evaluations of localization methods
by offering a comprehensive dataset and evaluation framework
that captures real-world environments.

  • Nodes

    40 Raspberry Pi Zero 2W hosting a Qorvo DWM3001CDK UWB module (IEEE 802.15.4z compliant).

    Time-of-Flight (ToF) measurements and reception statistics between poller and responder nodes.

    12 scan cycles conducted with random node placement, comprising 11 training and 1 evaluation scan.

  • Ground truth

    3D coordinates recorded using a Leica BLK360 Imaging Laser Scanner.
